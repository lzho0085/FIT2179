Key Insights

While all states show a drop in Manga interest, urban hubs like Kuala Lumpur exhibit the steepest overall decline.

Interestingly, regions like Labuan and Terengganu maintain the highest absolute interest across both categories. This indicates that while Anime dominates nationwide, the cultural retention of Manga varies significantly by local demographics.
